Wide Spectrum Behavioral Sensor

“Orage is an AI which analyses a large space of possibility to find systems capable of universal computation. By keeping track of the long range behavior of the signalling space and what were the antecedent signals that lead to that signal phase change (moving from one class to another) we can then prompt the user via Reinforcement Learning (Schedules of Reinforcement) back into the class 4 signaling space or into other class of signaling to extinguish behavior.”
